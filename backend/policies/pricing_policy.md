# SenAI Pricing Policy

## Overview
SenAI offers flexible pricing tiers designed for businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprises.

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter Plan - $49/month
- Up to 10,000 emails/month
- Basic email classification
- Standard support (24h response)
- 1 user account
- Data retention: 30 days
- API rate limit: 100 req/min

### Professional Plan - $149/month
- Up to 50,000 emails/month
- Advanced AI classification with custom rules
- Priority support (2h response)
- 5 user accounts
- Data retention: 90 days
- API rate limit: 1000 req/min
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Custom integrations

### Enterprise Plan - Custom pricing
- Unlimited emails
- White-label solution
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 phone support
- Unlimited user accounts
- Unlimited data retention
- API rate limit: 10000 req/min
- Custom SLA (99.95% uptime)
- On-premises deployment option

## Billing & Payment

- Monthly billing cycle starts on signup date
- Automatic renewal unless cancelled 7 days before end of cycle
- Accepted payment methods: Credit card, bank transfer, ACH
- Invoice generated on first day of billing cycle
- Late payment: 1.5% monthly interest charge
- Refund policy: Pro-rata refunds for unused portion if cancelled mid-cycle

## Discounts & Promotions

- Annual commitment: 15% discount
- Non-profit organizations: 50% discount
- Educational institutions: 60% discount
- Startup program: Free for first 3 months (if < $1M ARR)

## Price Changes

- 30 days notice required for price increases
- Current customers grandfathered for 12 months
- Applies to new customers immediately

## Usage Overage

- Overage rate: $0.002 per email (Starter/Professional plans)
- Enterprise accounts: included in contract
- Monthly overage cap: 10% of plan limit

## Free Trial

- 14-day free trial for all plans
- Full feature access during trial
- Credit card required for signup
- Trial converts to paid plan automatically unless cancelled

## Cancellation

- Cancel anytime through dashboard
- Pro-rata refund for unused portion
- Data export available for 30 days after cancellation
- No cancellation fees
