# SenAI Refund Policy

## General Refund Policy

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
- All paid plans eligible
- Full refund if cancelled within 30 days of subscription
- No questions asked
- Applies to first month only

### Pro-Rata Refunds
- Cancel mid-month: Refund for unused days
- Calculated as: (Days remaining / Days in month) × Monthly fee
- Applied automatically to original payment method

## Refund Eligibility

### Refunds ARE Provided For:
- Duplicate billing (accidental double-charge)
- Service downtime exceeding SLA for 3+ consecutive days
- Features not working as advertised
- Overages charged due to system error
- Unauthorized charges (fraud)

### Refunds ARE NOT Provided For:
- Change of mind after 30 days
- Unused portions of annual plans (after month 1)
- Discounted promo codes if terms not met
- Third-party integration costs
- Custom development work

## Refund Process

1. **Request Refund**
   - Email: billing@senai.io
   - Include: Account email, reason, transaction ID
   - Response time: 48 hours

2. **Approval**
   - Auto-approved if within 30-day window
   - Manual review if outside 30 days
   - Approval/denial notification via email

3. **Processing**
   - Refund issued to original payment method
   - Processing time: 3-5 business days
   - Bank may take additional 1-2 business days

## Special Cases

### Annual Plan Cancellation
- First 30 days: Full refund
- After 30 days: Pro-rata refund for remaining months
- Non-refundable fees: Payment processing fee (2%)

### Plan Downgrades
- Downgrade effective next billing cycle
- No refund for current month
- Credits applied if downgrading mid-month

### Free Trial Conversion
- If trial converts and charges within 24h: Full refund available
- Trial users not charged if not explicitly converted

### Fraud/Chargeback
- Disputed charges: Account suspended during investigation
- If determined customer error: Account reactivated, chargeback fee $15
- If determined company error: Full refund + $20 credit

## Enterprise Refunds

- Governed by signed contract
- Standard 30-day window may be extended
- Refund negotiations handled by account manager
- Multi-year contracts: Non-refundable after 30 days

## Appeal Process

- Disagree with refund decision?
- File appeal within 14 days
- Review by finance team
- Email: disputes@senai.io
- Response time: 5 business days

## Compliance

This refund policy complies with:
- FTC refund guidelines
- State consumer protection laws
- Credit card processor requirements (Stripe, Square)
- GDPR withdrawal of service rights (EU customers)
